Looking Ahead

Career aspirations

My goal is to grow into a front-end developer who cares as much about how an interface feels to use as how clean the code behind it is. In the next few years I want to work on products that make everyday tasks — like the ones student life throws at me — genuinely simpler.

Longer term, I'm interested in accessibility-focused design: building websites that work well for people using screen readers, keyboards, or slower connections, not just the easiest case.
